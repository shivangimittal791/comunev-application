About the Event

Diplomathon Global - World's Most Loved Student Platform on Experiential Learning is back with a delightful opportunity for every student in school to debate, discuss and brainstorm on international relations & current affairs. We are delighted to announce our first Virtual Intern School Conference: Diplomathon TM Global Virtual Edition to be held 6 & 7 June inviting thousands of students across the World to meet like-minded learners across geographies from the comfort of YOUR HOME!


Having influenced the lives of over 600K students in India & Internationally, Diplomathon Global is the Highhest Ranked Platform in India by Education World Eduresources Star Rankings 2019-2020 in Experiential Education for Programme Design, Training Support , Soft Skills & Value for Money. Establishing exclusive invitation to schools & students across the world for this unique experience of Model UN & similar Mock Parliamentary Simulations of organisations of global importance, it is a parliamentary form of debate on international organisations to understand real world scenarios and skill development. Diplomation Gloabal is a solitary stage where millennials meet diplomacy.


This Virtual conference shall be attended by students of Grades 6 to Grade 12 bringing everyone together on one platform to engage & interact with each other across the world from their respective Homes.
